Brownie

    Ingredient.


  • 170g chocolate
  • 300g sugar
  • 60g cocoa powder
  • 140g flour
  • 110g (1 bar) butter
  • a pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs

    Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F (175 °C). Mix melt chocolate and sugar. Stir in eggs and melt butter.


    Step 2

    Mix flour, cocoa poweder, and a pinch of salt. Beat in 3 portions.


    Step 3

    Bake in preheated oven for 35-40 minutes


Chocolate chip cookie

    Ingredient.


  • 240g brown sugar
  • 120g sugar
  • 240g (2 bars) butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 400g flour
  • 10g salt
  • 5g soda

    Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350 °F (175 °C). Mix melt butter and sugar, and Stir in eggs.


    Step 2

    Beat the flour in 3 portions. Mix the dough with chocolate chips.


    Step 3

    Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes

涼糕

    Ingredient.


  • 50g sugar
  • 450g water
  • 150g potato powder

    Step 1

    Fully mix all the ingredient in cold water.


    Step 2

    Small heat and keep stiring until it become static.


    Step 3

    Steam 15-20 minutes, then freeze it up to 4 hours.

Double layer pudding

    Ingredient.


  • 50g*3 sugar
  • 15cc water
  • 15cc hot water
  • 450cc warm milk
  • 25g butter
  • 7 eggs
  • 70g flour

    Step 1 - Bottom layer

    Mix 15cc cold water with 50g sugar. After boiling, add 15cc hot water.


    Step 2 - Mid layer

    Mix 4 eggs with 50g sugar. Stir warm milk in 3 portions and add vanilla(optional). Fliter.


    Step 3 - Top layer

    Mix 3 eggs with 50g sugar. Stir flour and melt butter.


    Step 4

    Water bath in oven. 340 °F (170 °C). 40-45 minutes.
